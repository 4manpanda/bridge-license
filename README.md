## 🧐 What is this?

The **BRIDGE (Bridge-Retention Infrastructure & Development General Executive) License** is a lightweight, behavior-binding fork of the classic MIT License. 

It keeps 99% of the freedom, open collaboration, and liability disclaimers you know and love, but adds a crucial **"Anti-Backstab Firewall"** patch.
